Residential Conveyancing Services

Moving home is one of life's biggest transactions. Our experienced conveyancing team makes the process as smooth and stress-free as possible.

Whether you're buying your first home, selling a property, or remortgaging, we provide efficient, professional conveyancing services with clear communication every step of the way.

Buying a Property

Our comprehensive purchase conveyancing service includes:

  • Reviewing the contract and title documents
  • Conducting property searches
  • Advising on survey results and legal matters
  • Liaising with mortgage lenders
  • Handling exchange of contracts
  • Completing the purchase and registration
  • Arranging Stamp Duty Land Tax payment

Selling a Property

When selling your property, we handle:

  • Preparing the contract pack
  • Dealing with buyer's enquiries
  • Negotiating terms where needed
  • Exchange and completion arrangements
  • Paying off existing mortgages
  • Transferring sale proceeds to you

Remortgaging

Looking for a better mortgage deal? We can help with:

  • Transfer of equity
  • Remortgage applications
  • Release of equity
  • Buy-to-let mortgages

New Build Properties

Buying a new build property requires specialist knowledge. We advise on:

  • NHBC warranties and developer guarantees
  • Build stage payments
  • Snagging issues
  • Leasehold considerations

The Conveyancing Process

1. Instruction

We gather your details and obtain initial information from the seller or buyer.

2. Due Diligence

We conduct searches and review all legal documents thoroughly.

3. Exchange

Once all parties are ready, contracts are exchanged and the sale becomes legally binding.

4. Completion

The balance is paid, keys are released, and you can move into your new home.
