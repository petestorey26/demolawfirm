Family Law

Compassionate support through difficult times

Expert Family Law Advice

Family matters require sensitivity, understanding, and expert legal knowledge. Our dedicated family law team provides practical advice with a compassionate approach.

We understand that family law matters can be emotionally challenging. Our solicitors combine legal expertise with empathy to help you navigate this difficult time and achieve the best possible outcome for you and your family.

Divorce and Separation

We guide you through every stage of divorce proceedings, including:

  • Issuing divorce petitions
  • Responding to divorce applications
  • Negotiating financial settlements
  • Judicial separation
  • Civil partnership dissolution
  • Mediation and alternative dispute resolution

Financial Settlements

Fair division of assets is crucial. We advise on:

  • Financial disclosure requirements
  • Division of property and assets
  • Pension sharing orders
  • Spousal maintenance
  • Consent orders
  • Protecting business interests

Children Matters

The welfare of children is always paramount. We provide advice on:

  • Child arrangement orders (residence and contact)
  • Parental responsibility
  • Child maintenance
  • Relocation and international issues
  • Special guardianship orders
  • Adoption

Pre-nuptial and Post-nuptial Agreements

Protect your assets with properly drafted agreements:

  • Pre-nuptial agreements before marriage
  • Post-nuptial agreements during marriage
  • Cohabitation agreements for unmarried couples
  • Separation agreements

Domestic Abuse

If you're experiencing domestic abuse, we can help with:

  • Non-molestation orders
  • Occupation orders
  • Emergency protective measures
  • Injunctions
Mediation First

Before starting court proceedings, you'll usually need to attend a Mediation Information and Assessment Meeting (MIAM). We can advise you on this process and represent you in mediation if appropriate.

Our Approach

We recognize that every family situation is unique. Our approach includes:

  • Clear communication: We explain legal processes in plain English
  • Cost awareness: We're upfront about costs and offer fixed fees where possible
  • Alternative resolution: We promote mediation and negotiation where appropriate
  • Court representation: Strong advocacy when court proceedings are necessary
  • Ongoing support: We're here to answer your questions throughout
