Wills & Probate

Protecting your legacy and supporting your loved ones

Expert Will Writing and Probate Services

Making a will is one of the most important things you can do to protect your family and ensure your wishes are carried out after you're gone.

At Demo Law, we understand that discussing wills and probate can be difficult. Our experienced solicitors provide compassionate, clear guidance through every step of the process.

Will Writing Services

A properly drafted will ensures your estate is distributed according to your wishes and can help minimize inheritance tax liability. Our will writing services include:

  • Single and mirror wills for couples
  • Trust wills to protect assets
  • Will reviews and updates
  • Advice on inheritance tax planning
  • Guardianship arrangements for children
  • Business succession planning

Probate Administration

Dealing with the estate of a loved one can be overwhelming. We offer comprehensive probate services to help executors and administrators:

  • Applying for grants of probate or letters of administration
  • Valuing and collecting estate assets
  • Paying debts and liabilities
  • Preparing estate accounts
  • Distributing the estate to beneficiaries
  • Handling inheritance tax matters

Contested Wills and Disputes

We also provide advice and representation in contentious probate matters, including:

  • Challenging the validity of a will
  • Claims under the Inheritance Act 1975
  • Executor disputes and removal applications
  • Professional negligence claims
